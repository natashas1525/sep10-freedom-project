Design art sofware + hardware

Software, digital programs for any needed information, creativity, and learning. Software is defined as the virtual programs that run on your computer; that is, operating system, internet browser, word-processing documents, etc.Software not only makes your computer hardware perform important tasks, but can also help your business work more efficiently. Software is known to carry out the input, processing, output, storage, and control activities.

Input

data that is entered into or received by hardware or software

Processing

the set of activities and associated outcomes that produce a software product.

Output

Data sent or delivered back to hardware or software

Storage

provides volume management, RAID protection, and LUN masking

LUN masking

a process of hiding or masking certain logical unit numbers (LUNs) from specific hosts or servers. A LUN is a logical representation of a physical storage device or a portion of it, such as a disk, a volume, or a RAID group.

RAID

RAID (redundant array of independent disks) is a way of storing the same data in different places on multiple hard disks or solid-state drives (SSDs) to protect data in the case of a drive failure.

Control

the process of the physical storage in a definitive software library of all software to ensure that only correctly released, licensed, and authorized versions of software are in use. Processes in this category include the tracking of releases, patches, service packs, and bug fixes.

Hardware, the physical tools, machinery, and other durable equipment. Without hardware, there would be no way of running the essential software that makes computers so useful. Hardware refers to the external and internal devices and equipment that enable you to perform major functions such as input, output, storage, communication, processing, and more.

Communications

any type of hardware capable of transmitting data, instructions, and information between a sending device and a receiving device.
There are two types of computer hardware: external and internal.

External Hardware

External hardware components, also called peripheral components, are items that are often externally connected to the computer to control either input or output functions.
peripheral components
Examples of peripheral devices (aka external hardware) include terminals, printers, external floppy disk drives and other data storage devices, video monitors, keyboards, interface boards, external memory expansion cards, and other input/output devices that may or may not contain digital circuitry.

Internal Hardware

Internal hardware includes components such as memory, a video card, or a CPU
CPU
the primary component of a computer that acts as its “control center.”
Video card
a printed circuit board controlling output to a display screen.
